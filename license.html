Medical Disclaimer

The Informed Patient Project does not provide medical advice. All content is for informational and educational purposes only.

The resources provided by The Informed Patient Project are intended to support communication between patients and their healthcare providers. They are not intended to serve as medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or clinical guidelines.

All medical decisions — including diagnosis, treatment, medication, and lifestyle changes — should be made in consultation with a qualified, licensed healthcare professional who knows your individual health history.

The information in our resources is based on published peer-reviewed research and established medical guidelines at the time of creation. Medical knowledge evolves; we update our resources when significant changes occur, but we cannot guarantee that all information reflects the most current clinical practice at the moment of your download.

The Informed Patient Project is not a medical organization, healthcare provider, or licensed clinical practice. Use of our resources does not establish any patient-provider relationship with The Informed Patient Project.

Citation Policy

All factual claims in our resources are supported by citations to peer-reviewed medical literature, established medical society publications, or clinical practice guidelines. Sources are listed within each PDF document.

We do not promote unproven treatments, pseudoscience, or claims that contradict established medical consensus. We advocate for better recognition and understanding of real, documented medical conditions.
